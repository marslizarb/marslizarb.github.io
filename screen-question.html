A Question of Sex

Scientific American, 2022

I created motion graphics for this documentary miniseries about the science of sex and gender.

mood board

We were working with extremely limited resources, so I needed to create a style that was economical yet visually stimulating.

We decided to go in a direction that combined sharp, minimal linework with soft, colorful gradients. This was meant to evoke the precision of medical illustrations in contrast with the nebulous quality of gender and sexuality.
