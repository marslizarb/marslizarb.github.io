Marcie LaCerte

Marcie is a Chinese-American experimental and editorial animator who lives in New York City. She currently creates motion graphics for "Explained" on Netflix and is also working on a film, probably.

She is interested in creating work that examines human psychology through a humorous, surreal lens. She is also interested in text-based games, sketch comedy, and filmmaking. In her free time, she enjoys thrift shopping and watching extremely long videos on YouTube about pop culture.

Please send very good content to marcielacerte@gmail.com.
