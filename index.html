Marcie LaCerte

Marcie is a Chinese-American experimental and editorial animator based in Manhattan. She is currently a Creative Culture fellow at the Jacob Burns Film Center and is working on a short film. Most recently, she worked as an animator for season three of Explained on Netflix.

She is interested in making films that use metaphor, surrealism, and comedy to examine personal experiences and feelings. She is also interested in web-based multimedia art and the creative potential of the internet. In her free time, she enjoys thrift shopping, listening to podcasts, and talking about Shrek.

Clients include Giphy, School of Life, NPR, Quartz, Vox, Rope Line Media, Edutopia, and more.
