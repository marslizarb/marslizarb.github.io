ABOUT

Marcie LaCerte is a recent animation graduate from the Minneapolis College of Art and Design. She's interested in making short films, long films, weird games, and weird websites. But most of her time is spent watching videos on the Internet.

She is available for freelance work and can be reached at marcielacerte@gmail.com.
