about me

Marcie LaCerte is a Chinese-American experimental and editorial animator based in NYC. She is currently a Creative Culture fellow at the Jacob Burns Film Center and is working on a narrative short film. She works as a motion designer and has created graphics for Netflix, NPR, Vox, Giphy, Quartz, and more.

She is interested in making films that use metaphor, surrealism, and comedy to examine personal experiences and feelings. She is also interested in web-based multimedia art and interactive storytelling. In her free time, she enjoys looking at vintage clothing, listening to podcasts, and talking about Shrek.
